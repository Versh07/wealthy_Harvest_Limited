About us

Wealthy Harvest Limited is a forward-thinking agribusiness company established in 2024 with a clear vision: to transform agriculture into a profitable, sustainable, and impactful venture in Nigeria and beyond.

Our company is built on the foundation of passion for food security, economic empowerment, and rural development.

We are a proudly Nigerian-owned company, fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in 2024, and legally recognized to carry out agricultural and related commercial activities across the country.

Wealthy Harvest Limited is owned and operated by a dedicated team of seven visionary stakeholders, each bringing unique expertise, capital investment, and shared commitment to driving innovation in agriculture. Our stakeholders come from diverse professional backgrounds, which enriches our decision-making process and enhances the effectiveness of our operations.
