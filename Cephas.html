Afolabi Olabisi Cephas is a dynamic and purpose-driven stakeholder at Wealthy Harvest Limited. Born on May 12, and hailing from Atiba Local Government Area of Oyo State, Cephas embodies the spirit of innovation, dedication, and faith that defines the vision of the company.

With an academic background in Quantity Surveying (HND), Cephas brings a solid foundation in planning, analysis, and resource management—key qualities essential for the growth and sustainability of any agribusiness. Beyond his technical expertise, he is also skilled in fashion design and other technical and vocational skills, which reflect his creativity, discipline, and hands-on approach to problem-solving.

A devoted Christian and committed learner, Cephas values prayer, personal study, and continuous development. His calm yet visionary presence contributes to the spiritual and strategic direction of the company, fostering a culture rooted in integrity, excellence, and shared growth.

Cephas currently resides in FCT Abuja, where he is actively involved in various facets of personal development and team-building initiatives. His commitment to the mission of Wealthy Harvest Limited is both inspiring and impactful.

“I'm glad to be part of this team,” he says—a statement that reflects his humility, loyalty, and strong belief in the future of agriculture and the transformative power of teamwork.

As Wealthy Harvest Limited continues to scale new heights in large-scale farming and agribusiness, Cephas remains a valuable contributor to the company’s success and sustainability.
